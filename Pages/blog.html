The Coastal Journal

The tourism sector is rebounding faster than ever, and land value in Msambweni and Diani is reflecting this surge.

With the expansion of the Diani airport and improved road networks connecting the coast to the Lunga Lunga border, accessibility has reached an all-time high. For investors, this means higher capital appreciation and rental yields for holiday homes.

Whether you are looking for a retirement home or a commercial plot, the window for sub-10 million KES beach-access plots is narrowing quickly.

Understanding the difference between Freehold and Leasehold is vital for any property buyer in Kenya.

Most agricultural land in Kwale County is held under Freehold titles, while beach-front properties and commercial zones often fall under 99-year Leaseholds. At Silicon Properties, we ensure every title deed is verified through the lands registry before any commitment is made.

Our legal team assists in the search process, ensuring your investment is secure from day one.

How to design homes that breathe in the tropical humidity without relying solely on Air Conditioning.

Using high ceilings, makuti roofing, and local Galana stone flooring can reduce the internal temperature of a house by up to 5 degrees. We explore how modern Swahili-style architecture is evolving to include smart home features while maintaining its cultural soul.
